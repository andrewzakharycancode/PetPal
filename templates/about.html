About PetPal

At PetPal, our mission is to provide a comprehensive platform that helps animal shelters and rescues manage their residents while connecting them with the resources they need. We're dedicated to ensuring every pet receives the best care possible.

Quick Reference

  • Track Your Shelter's and Rescue's Guests: Easily monitor the health and well-being of all pets in your shelter or rescue.
  • Find Vet Referrals: Connect with top-rated veterinarians in your area for your guests' needs.
  • Partner with Resource Providers: Access a network of high-quality pet product and service providers to support your shelter or rescue.

Get Involved

Your support is crucial to our mission. To make a donation, report animal abuse, or share any other concerns, please visit our Contact Us page. Together, we can make a difference for pets in need.
